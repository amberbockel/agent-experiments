# Product Launch Checklist

**Category:** business  
**One-liner:** Track everything you need to launch a new product — photos, pricing, listings, marketing — with a reusable checklist that saves progress and celebrates when you ship.

## What it does

Launching a new product (print, POD item, workshop, digital download) takes 20+ steps: product photos, pricing, product description, shop listing, social posts, email announcement, etc. This tool gives you a structured checklist organized by phase (Create → List → Market → Launch) with progress tracking and localStorage persistence.

## Who it's for

Amber (Amber Miro Creative, AgentFleur), or anyone launching creative products regularly who needs a reusable system to make sure nothing gets forgotten.

## Key features

- **4 phases:** Create, List, Market, Launch
- **20+ pre-loaded tasks** covering the full launch lifecycle
- **Progress tracking:** Visual progress ring + percentage
- **localStorage:** Save your progress, pick up where you left off
- **Reusable:** Reset when done, start the next launch fresh
- **Mobile-first:** Check things off from your phone

## Visual direction

Clean iOS base with a bold creative accent (teal or coral). Progress ring front-and-center. Feels organized but not corporate — this is a creative business tool.
