# Magic Decision Maker

**Category:** delightful  
**One-liner:** Mystical toy for trivial decisions — ask a question, shake your phone, get delightfully dramatic guidance with personality.

## What it does

A digital oracle for everyday choices. Should you get coffee? Order takeout? Watch another episode? The Magic Decision Maker delivers answers with theatrical flair and just enough personality to make mundane decisions fun.

## How it works

1. Type your question (or pick a suggested one)
2. Shake your phone (or tap the orb)
3. Watch the mystical reveal animation
4. Get an answer with personality — supportive, playful, or gently sarcastic
5. Ask again (decisions are hard)

## Why it exists

Sometimes you need permission to do the thing you already want to do. Sometimes you need a nudge. Sometimes you just need a laugh. This is for those moments — delightful, tactile, zero stakes.

## Target audience

Amber, William, Zev — anyone who overthinks small decisions or just wants a playful ritual.

## Visual vibe

Mystical meets iOS — deep purple gradients, gold accents, soft glow effects, crystal ball aesthetic. More personality than flat iOS default, but still clean and modern.

## Tech

- Single HTML file, inline CSS/JS
- DeviceMotion API for shake detection
- localStorage for question history
- Mobile-first, safe-area-inset aware
- ~20KB total
