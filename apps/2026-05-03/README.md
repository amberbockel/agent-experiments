# Workshop Pricing Calculator

**Category:** Business  
**Built:** 2026-05-03  
**One-liner:** Price your workshops with confidence — calculate break-even, competitive, and premium tiers based on your time, materials, and desired income.

## What it does

Helps creators and educators price workshops accurately by accounting for all the hidden costs:

- Prep time (designing curriculum, materials)
- Teaching time (actual workshop duration)
- Materials cost per student
- Platform fees (Zoom, Teachable, Eventbrite)
- Desired hourly rate

Returns three pricing tiers:
1. **Break-even** — covers costs, no profit
2. **Competitive** — 30% margin, fair market rate
3. **Premium** — 50% margin, high-value positioning

## Why it exists

Amber teaches painting workshops. Pricing is hard when you factor in prep, materials, platform fees, and your own expertise. Most people undercharge because they forget to count prep time or don't build in margin.

This calculator makes pricing transparent and defensible.

## Design notes

- Clean, confident business aesthetic
- Numbers are the hero — tabular figures, clear hierarchy
- iOS base but with a slightly more formal/professional vibe
- Dark mode for late-night planning sessions
- Saves inputs to localStorage so you can reference past workshops
