# Outreach Tracker

**Category:** Productivity  
**Date:** 2026-05-12  
**One-liner:** Track who you've reached out to, when, and why — log pitches, partnerships, and collaborations with smart follow-up reminders so nothing falls through the cracks.

## The Problem

When you're running multiple businesses and reaching out to people across Substack, workshops, partnerships, press, and collaborations, it's easy to lose track:
- Did I follow up with that gallery owner?
- When did I pitch that podcast?
- Who owes me a response?
- What was the last thing I sent them?

Email threads get buried. DMs scatter across platforms. Follow-ups slip.

## The Solution

A dead-simple mobile tracker for logging outreach and managing follow-ups:

- **Log each contact** — name, context (what you're pitching), date reached out
- **Status tracking** — awaiting reply, in progress, done, no response
- **Auto follow-up reminders** — see who you contacted 7+ days ago with no reply
- **Quick notes** — jot down context so you remember what you said
- **Filter and sort** — by status, by date, by person
- **Local storage** — all data stays on device, no account needed

## Design

Clean flat iOS style — this is productivity infrastructure, not brand content.

- System font stack
- Card-based list view with generous tap targets
- Soft shadows, rounded corners (12px)
- iOS blue accent (#007AFF)
- Light mode default, dark mode support
- Tabular figures for dates
- Mobile-first, single-column layout

## Target Users

1. **Amber** — tracking Substack collab pitches, workshop partnerships, gallery outreach
2. **Freelancers** — managing client leads and proposal follow-ups
3. **Anyone juggling multiple projects** — who needs a simple system to not drop the ball

## Success Metrics

- Amber uses it daily for at least one week
- Catches at least one follow-up that would have slipped
- Takes <10 seconds to log a new contact
