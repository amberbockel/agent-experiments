# Creative Product Pricer

**Category:** business  
**One-liner:** Price your creative work with confidence across originals, prints, licensing, and workshops — calculate value-based rates using materials, time, experience, and market positioning.

## What it does

Artists and creatives often underprice their work. This tool walks you through a framework to price:
- Original artwork
- Limited edition prints
- Open edition prints
- Licensing deals
- Workshops/classes

Uses a value-based model: materials + time + experience multiplier + market positioning.

## Why it matters

Amber Miro Creative ships paintings, prints, and workshops. Pricing isn't just cost-plus — it's about positioning, scarcity, and experience. This tool makes those calculations explicit and repeatable.

## How it works

1. Set your base rate ($/hour based on experience level)
2. Pick a product type
3. Enter materials cost + hours invested
4. Choose market positioning (emerging / established / premium)
5. Get a calculated price range with breakdown

Stores your base rate in localStorage so you don't re-enter it.

## Technical

- Single HTML file, inline CSS + JS
- localStorage for base rate persistence
- Mobile-first (iPhone safe areas)
- ~18KB total
- Clean iOS aesthetic with artist-friendly warm accent color

## Design notes

Flat iOS default with a warm ochre accent (`#D4A574`) — feels professional but creative. Cards, generous spacing, clear hierarchy. Dark mode not needed for a pricing tool that gets used in working hours.
